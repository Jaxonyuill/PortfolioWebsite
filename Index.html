My Journey

My passion for programming began at age 14-16 when I was managing Minecraft servers with upwards of 30,000 players. This experience sparked my love for coding as I developed solutions to help players and manage the massive community. I enjoyed talking to people and coming up with innovative solutions to improve their experience, all while doing this for free. This early exposure to problem-solving at scale solidified my desire to pursue software development.
